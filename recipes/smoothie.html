Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

A delectable slice of Tiramisu!

This recipe is rather simple to make and will impress all of your dinner guests!

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks/li>
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste/li>
  • 2 cups ice cubes
    1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
    2. Step 2:Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender.
    3. Step 3: Blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
    4. Step 4:Enjoy!
